My integral being, dhibhuta (the
field of matter), Adhidaiva (Brahma) andAdhiyajn
(the unmanifest Divinity dwelling in the heart
of all beings as their witness). And they who,
possessed of a steadfast mind, know thus
even at the hour of death, they too know Me
alone. (29-30)
