The other path is that wherein are stationed
the gods presiding over smoke, night, the
dark fortnight, and the six months of the
southward course of the sun; the Yogi (devoted
to action with an interested motive) taking to
this path after death is led by the above gods,
one after another, and attaining the lustre of
the moon (and enjoying the fruit of this
meritorious deeds in heaven) returns to this
mortal world. (25)
