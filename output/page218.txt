28.

CHAPTER-VIII 185

(Dampati Devata, Atri Rshi)

wig दर्शमास्यो गर्भो जरार्युणा सह | यथायं वायुरेर्जंति
यर्था समुद्रऽएर्जति | एवायं दर्शमास्योऽअरस्स्रज्जरार्युणा
सह ॥२८॥

Ejatu dasamasyo garbho jarayuna saha. Yatha’-
yam vayurejati yatha samudra’ ejati. Evayam
dasamasyo’ asrajjarayuna saha.

May the foetus of the tenth month stir with its

membrane-cover just as this wind moves, just as the
sea moves. And may this foetus of the tenth month be
born (safe and alive) with the membrane cover.

29.

(Dampati Devata, Atri Rshi)

यस्यै a यज्ञियो गर्भो यस्यै योर्निर्हिरण्ययीं |
अङ्गान्य्नुता यस्य तं मात्रा सर्मजीगमश्स्वाहां ॥२९॥
Yasyai te yajniyo garbho yasyai yonirhiranyayl.
Anganyahruta yasya tam matra samajigamam
svaha.

Dear wife, for whom your sacred foetus is meant,

for whom the pure and perfect womb of yours is meant,
by that mother — you partner — may I beget a child
with full and perfect limbs of the body through the
sacred procreative yajna.
