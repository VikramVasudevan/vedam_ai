द्यावापृथिवी वव्यैतां वर्सवो रुद्राऽरआदित्याऽरअनुव्पायँस्तऽ-
एवार्धिपतयऽआसन् ॥३०॥

Navadasabhirastuvata sudraryavasrjyeta-
mahoratre’adhipatni’astam. Ekavimsatyastu-
vataikasaphah pasavo’srjyanta varuno’-
dhipatirasit. TrayovimSatyastuvata ksudrah
pasavo’srjyanta pusa’dhipatirasit. Pancaviin-
Satya’stuvata”ranyah pasavo’ srjyanta
vayuradhipatirasit. Saptavimsatyda’stuvata
dyavaprthivi vyaitam vasavo rudrda’aditya’ -
anuvyayansta’ evadhipataya’ asan.

With nineteen (ten pranas, five elements, mind,
reason/intellect, memory and self-consciousness),
worship Him who created the educated and the
uneducated all, with the day-and-night cycle of time
for work and rest as the power of control over work and
rest.
