7.

(Vidvans Devata, Narayana Rshi)
दिष्टार्य woes gad मृगयुमन्तंकाय श्वनिर्नम् ॥७॥

Tapase kaulalam mayayai karmaram rupdaya
manikaram subhe vapain saravyaya’isukaram
hetyai dhanuskaram karmane jyakaram distaya
rajjusarjam mrtyave mrgayum antakaya
Svaninam.

For heat-treatment, the potter or metallurgist; for

furnishing, the decorator; for ornament, the jeweller;
for beauty, the beautician; for archery, the arrow-maker;
for shooting, the bow-maker; for the target, the bow-
string maker; for marking and delimitation, the string-
maker; for killing, the hunter; for the border, the watch-

dog.
8.

(Vidvans Devata, Narayana Rshi)

गन्धर्वाप्सरोभ्यो amet wargssah: सर्पदेवजनेभ्योऽ
प्रतिपदमयेंभ्य: कितवमीर्यतायाऽअर्कितवं पिशाचेभ्यों
विदलकारीं यांतुधार्नेभ्य: कण्टकीकारीम् ॥८॥

Nadibhyah paunjisthamrksikabhyo naisadam
purusavyadghraya durmadam gandharva-
psarobhyo vratyam prayugbhya’unmattamn
sarpadevajanebhyo’ pratipadam ayebhyah
kitavamiryataya’akitavam pisacebhyo
bidalakarim yatudhanebhyah kantakikarim.
