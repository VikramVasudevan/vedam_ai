908 YAJURVEDA

CHAPTER-XXXI

1. (Purusha Devata, Narayana Rshi)

सहस्रशीर्षा Gea: सहस्राक्ष: aeetur |
स भूरमिंश add स्पृत्वाऽर्त्यतिष्ठद्दृशाङ्गुलम् ॥१॥

Sahasrasirsa purusah sahasraksah sahasrapat.
Sa bhumiim sarvata sprtva’tyatisthaddas-
angulam.

Purusha, the Cosmic Soul of Existence, is the soul
of the universe of a thousand heads, a thousand eyes
and a thousand feet. It pervades the universe wholly
and entirely and, pervading and sustaining the universe
of ten constituents of living Prakriti, It transcends the
world of existence.

2. (Ishvara Devata, Narayana Rshi)

उतार्मृतत्वस्येशानो  aadarfatefa ॥२॥
Purusa’evedam sarvam yadbhitam yacca

bhavyam. Utamrtatvasyesano yadannendati-
rohati.

All this that is and was and shall be is Purusha
ultimately, sovereign over immortality and ruler of what
grows by living food.

3. (Purusha Devata, Narayana Rshi)

एतार्वानस्य महिमातो ज्यायाश्च gee: |
पार्दोऽस्य favat spar त्रिपार्दस्यामूर्ते दिवि ॥३॥
Etavanasya mahimato jyayansca purusah. Pado’
sya visva bhutani tripadasyamrtam divi.
