The recitation, hearing of exploits of
Sri Rama bestow four objects—Kama—the
gratification of desire, Artha—acquisition of
wealth, Dharma—discharge of duty and
Moksa—final emancipation. So one should
listen Ramayana carefully and diligently.

श्रृण्वन् रामायणं भक्त्या य: पादं पदमेव वा।

स याति ब्रह्मण: स्थानं ब्रह्मणा पूज्यते सदा॥ २४॥
Listening even a syllable or quarter of

verse of the Ramayana with reverence, one

attains the world of Brahma and is regarded

and honoured there by Brahma. (24)

एवमेतत् पुराव्रत्तमाख्यानं भद्रमस्तु asl
प्रव्याहरत fret act विष्णो: प्रवर्धताम्॥ २५॥
Thus recite this historical narrative with

full faith. May all be well with you and enhance
power of Lord Visnu (Sri Rama). (25)

इत्यार्षे श्रीमद्रामायणे वाल्मीकीये आदिकाव्ये उत्तरकाण्डे एकादशाधिकशततम: सर्ग: ॥ १११ ॥

Ramayana of Valmiki, the work of a Rsi and the oldest epic.
