“You are Indra,” said he, “the king of the
celestials: we are but mortals, O lord of the
world: you must pardon therefore the offence
that I have committed. Let this Parijata tree be
taken to its appropriate situation. I removed it
in compliance with the words of Satya.
Receive back also this your thunderbolt, cast
at me; for this is your proper weapon, the
destroyer of your foes.”
