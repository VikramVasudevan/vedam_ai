O Vyasa in the form of Vishnu, 0 Vishnu in the form of Vyasa, 0
Vasishta, the treasure of the Brahman, I offer my obeisances
unto Thee again and again.

अविकाराय शुद्धाय नित्याय WRA |
सदैकरूपरूपाय विष्णवे सर्वजिष्णवे ॥ ५॥

avikaraya Suddhaya nityaya paramatmane,
sadaikaruparupaya visnave sarvajisnave. (5)
Bow I before Vishnu, Who is pure, Who is not affected, Who is

permanent, Who is the ultimate truth, and He who wins over all
the mortals in this world.

यस्य स्मरणमात्रेण जन्मसंसारबन्धनात् |
विमुच्यते नमस्तस्मै विष्णवे प्रभविष्णवे ॥६॥

yasya smaranamatrena janmasamsarabamdhanat,
vimucyate namastasmai visnave prabhavisnave. (6)

Obeisance to that Vishnu, the all-powerful Vishnu, whose
remembrance alone frees one from the bondage of birth and
death.
