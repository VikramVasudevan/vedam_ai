तमेव चार्चयन्नित्यं भक्त्या पुरुषमव्ययम् |
ध्यायन् स्तुवन् नमस्यंश्च यजमानस्तमेव च ॥ ११॥

tameva carcayannityam bhaktya purusamavyayam,
dhyayan stuvannamasyamsca yajamanastameva Ca. (11)

And worshiping Him alone with devotion, meditating on the
inexhaustible Purusha, praising and bowing down to Him alone,
and offering sacrifices to Him alone, the worshipper, the
aspirant (is freed).

लोकाध्यक्षं स्तुवन्नित्यं सर्वदु:खातिगो भवेत् ॥ १२॥

anadinidhanam visnum sarvalokamahesvaram,
lokadhyaksam stuvannityam sarvaduhkhatigo bhavet. (12)

By praising Vishnu, the eternally immortal, the great Lord of all
the worlds, the presiding deity of the worlds, one can pass
beyond all sorrows.

लोकनाथं महद्भूतं सर्वभूतभवोद्भवम् ॥ १३॥

brahmanyam sarvadharmajiiam lokanam kirtivardhanam,
lokanatham mahadbhutam sarvabhittabhavodhbhavam. (13)

He is the Brahman, the knower of all religions, the enhancer of
the fame of the worlds, the Lord of the worlds, the great being,
the origin of all beings.
